About Me

Hello! My name is Brittany Stidd, and I'm a full stack developer based in Portland, Oregon. After growing up in Bend, I studied dental assisting on the coast, where I practiced for four years. I then moved into oral and maxillofacial surgery to become a surgical anesthesia assistant, where I thrived for nearly seven years and made so many amazing connections with patients as well as my coworkers who became family.

However, my passion for technology, learning, and software development led me to switch careers. After much contemplation, I decided to take the leap and switch into tech. I started learning programming languages and web development on my own and eventually enrolled in immersive. With my strong work ethic and determination, I was able to successfully transition into a career as a full stack developer.

I enjoy collaborating with others to brainstorm and implement new ideas while also being able to work independently to solve complex problems. My background in healthcare has taught me the importance of attention to detail and problem-solving -- skills that I carry with me into my work as a developer.

I'm passionate about creating efficient, user-friendly, and beautiful web applications that solve real-world problems. I'm excited to bring my diverse background and unique perspective to any team and create innovative solutions that exceed expectations.

Brittany Stidd
